What We Do

Hackers on the Hill puts hackers and policymakers in the same room to talk about cyber policy. We're an all-volunteer initiative from I Am The Cavalry. No one gets paid. No one's selling anything. We just think policy works better when the people writing it talk to the people who know how things break.

The Problem We're Solving

Hackers

Hackers are early adopters. We poke, ask questions, and see possibilities. We spot trends and see where things can go wrong — often years before others.

Policymakers

Policymakers steer society toward its values and away from harm. To do that well, they need to see far enough ahead to avoid unintended consequences.

The Bridge

But hackers and policymakers don't usually run in the same circles or speak the same language. So we introduce them.

A Global Movement

What started in Washington, DC has grown worldwide. Each event is organized by local cybersecurity professionals who bridge the gap between technical expertise and public policy in their region.
