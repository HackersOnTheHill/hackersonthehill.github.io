The Problem We're Solving

Hackers are early adopters. They explore, ask questions, and see possibilities. They spot trends and see where things can go wrong — often years before others.

Policymakers steer society toward its values and away from harm. To do that, they need forward-looking awareness to avoid unintended consequences.

But hackers and policymakers don't often have the same social circles or speak the same language. Hackers on the Hill connects them.

What We Do

Hackers on the Hill puts cybersecurity researchers and policymakers in the same room to talk about cyber policy. We're an all-volunteer initiative from I Am The Cavalry. No one gets paid and no one's selling anything. We just think policy works better when the people writing it talk to the people who know how things break.

A Global Movement

What started in Washington, DC has grown worldwide. Each event is organized by local cybersecurity professionals who bridge the gap between technical expertise and public policy in their region.

We're planning even more events in more places. Join our notification list to hear when that happens.
