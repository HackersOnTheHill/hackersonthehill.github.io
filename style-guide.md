<section class="style-guide-cheatsheet section-padding-sm">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="row justify-content-center">
      <div class="col-12 col-lg-9">
        <h2>Cheat Sheet</h2>
        <div class="style-guide-cheatsheet-card">
          <div class="style-guide-cheatsheet-grid">
            <div>
              <h3>The 5 Rules</h3>
              <ol class="style-guide-list">
                <li><strong>Legibility wins</strong> (works at sticker size and social avatar size).</li>
                <li><strong>Minimal beats busy</strong> (one idea per graphic).</li>
                <li><strong>Civic and modern</strong> (never cyber-edgy).</li>
                <li><strong>Black and white first</strong>; accents are deliberate (purple = structure, gold = action).</li>
                <li><strong>Local identity is welcome</strong>, but the family resemblance stays.</li>
              </ol>
            </div>
            <div>
              <h3>Avoid List</h3>
              <ul class="style-guide-list">
                <li>Stock photos (and generally photos).</li>
                <li>Circuits, code rain, glitch, neon cyber aesthetics.</li>
                <li>Hoodies, stereotypes, or hacker villain vibes.</li>
                <li>Handshakes, suits, corporate cliches.</li>
                <li>Locks, shields, eagles, gavels, flags as backdrops.</li>
                <li>Gradients, bevels, textures, heavy effects.</li>
                <li>Bright or loud palettes beyond a small highlight.</li>
              </ul>
            </div>
            <div class="style-guide-note">
              <h3>When in doubt</h3>
              <p>Choose clarity and neutrality over style flourishes. If it does not read instantly, simplify it.</p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="style-guide-section section-padding-sm" id="brand-vibe">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="row justify-content-center">
      <div class="col-12 col-lg-9">
        <h2>Brand vibe / anti-vibe</h2>
        <ul class="style-guide-summary">
          <li>Reads like a public-interest briefing: calm, credible, human.</li>
          <li>Neutral and non-partisan; no adversarial or edgy energy.</li>
          <li>One clear idea per asset; clarity beats cleverness.</li>
          <li>Flat, clean, and minimal with generous whitespace.</li>
        </ul>
        <details class="accordion-minimal">
          <summary>Details, rationale, checklist</summary>
          <div class="style-guide-details-body">
            <p>The brand should feel like civic service: modern, steady, and easy to parse for busy audiences. Aim for quiet confidence, not hype.</p>
            <div class="style-guide-callouts">
              <div class="style-guide-callout">
                <h4>Do</h4>
                <ul>
                  <li>Use calm, neutral language and visuals.</li>
                  <li>Use flat shapes and simple geometry.</li>
                  <li>Leave space around key messages.</li>
                </ul>
              </div>
              <div class="style-guide-callout">
                <h4>Don't</h4>
                <ul>
                  <li>Use cyber tropes or dramatic effects.</li>
                  <li>Over-decorate or add visual noise.</li>
                  <li>Imply partisanship or ideology.</li>
                </ul>
              </div>
            </div>
            <h3>No-go list (explicit)</h3>
            <ul>
              <li>Stock photos or photo-heavy layouts.</li>
              <li>Circuit traces, code rain, neon cyber visuals.</li>
              <li>Hacker stereotype imagery (hoodies, masks).</li>
              <li>Handshakes, corporate stock vibes, generic office shots.</li>
              <li>Locks, shields, eagles, gavels unless explicitly approved.</li>
              <li>Gradients, bevels, 3D, textures, heavy effects.</li>
              <li>Bright or loud palettes beyond a small highlight.</li>
            </ul>
            <h3>Checklist</h3>
            <ul class="style-guide-checklist">
              <li>Feels neutral, credible, and calm.</li>
              <li>One main idea, instantly readable.</li>
              <li>No visual cliches or cyber motifs.</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

        <details class="accordion-minimal">
          <summary>Details, rationale, checklist</summary>
          <div class="style-guide-details-body">
            <h3>Canonical tokens</h3>
            <div class="style-guide-color-grid">
              <div>

                </ul>
              </div>
              <div>
                <h4>Accents</h4>
                <ul class="style-guide-color-list">
                  <li><span class="style-guide-swatch" style="background-color: var(--rich-violet);"></span>Rich Violet <code>#6B46A3</code></li>

            <ul>

              <li>Local colors can appear as a single highlight element, never a full rainbow palette.</li>
              <li>Contrast matters more than style; make text and icons instantly readable.</li>
            </ul>
            <h3>Checklist</h3>
            <ul class="style-guide-checklist">
              <li>Neutrals are the base in every design.</li>
              <li>Accent colors feel deliberate, not decorative.</li>
              <li>Local colors stay small and focused.</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>
    </div>

<section class="style-guide-section section-padding-sm" id="typography">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="row justify-content-center">
      <div class="col-12 col-lg-9">
        <h2>Typography</h2>
        <ul class="style-guide-summary">
          <li>Manrope is primary; Noto Sans is the fallback.</li>
          <li>Stick to Regular, Medium, and SemiBold weights.</li>
          <li>Sentence case, normal letter spacing, readable sizes.</li>
        </ul>
        <details class="accordion-minimal">
          <summary>Details, rationale, checklist</summary>
          <div class="style-guide-details-body">
            <p>Typography should be calm and highly readable. Treat text as the primary design element in most assets.</p>
            <h3>Usage</h3>
            <ul>
              <li>Use sentence case for headers and body copy.</li>
              <li>Avoid all caps except for tiny labels or short tags.</li>
              <li>Keep line lengths short (around 60 to 70 characters).</li>
              <li>Prioritize contrast over stylistic flourishes.</li>
            </ul>
            <h3>Checklist</h3>
            <ul class="style-guide-checklist">
              <li>Text remains legible at small sizes.</li>
              <li>Weight usage is restrained and consistent.</li>
              <li>Copy feels neutral and informational.</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="style-guide-section section-padding-sm" id="iconography">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="row justify-content-center">
      <div class="col-12 col-lg-9">
        <h2>Iconography &amp; illustration style</h2>
        <ul class="style-guide-summary">
          <li>Flat, minimal icons with simple geometry.</li>
          <li>Consistent weight and clear meaning at a glance.</li>
          <li>No cliche hacker imagery or cartoon emoji style.</li>
        </ul>
        <details class="accordion-minimal">
          <summary>Details, rationale, checklist</summary>
          <div class="style-guide-details-body">
            <div class="style-guide-callouts">
              <div class="style-guide-callout">
                <h4>Do</h4>
                <ul>
                  <li>Use consistent stroke weights.</li>
                  <li>Keep shapes geometric and simple.</li>
                  <li>Prefer symbolic clarity over decoration.</li>
                </ul>
              </div>
              <div class="style-guide-callout">
                <h4>Don't</h4>
                <ul>
                  <li>Use emoji, clip art, or overly detailed scenes.</li>
                  <li>Reference hacker stereotypes or cyber warfare imagery.</li>
                  <li>Mix illustration styles in a single piece.</li>
                </ul>
              </div>
            </div>
            <h3>Checklist</h3>
            <ul class="style-guide-checklist">
              <li>Icons read clearly at small sizes.</li>
              <li>Line weight and style are consistent.</li>
              <li>No cliches or heavy visual effects.</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="style-guide-section section-padding-sm" id="composition">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="row justify-content-center">
      <div class="col-12 col-lg-9">
        <h2>Composition &amp; spacing</h2>
        <ul class="style-guide-summary">
          <li>Whitespace is a tool; keep layouts open and breathable.</li>
          <li>Use clear hierarchy and alignment for fast scanning.</li>
          <li>One message per asset; avoid competing focal points.</li>
        </ul>
        <details class="accordion-minimal">
          <summary>Details, rationale, checklist</summary>
          <div class="style-guide-details-body">
            <h3>Practical guidance</h3>
            <ul>
              <li>Align edges and baselines; avoid visual drift.</li>
              <li>Use scale to signal priority, not decoration.</li>
              <li>Limit accents to one or two elements per piece.</li>
              <li>Make the primary message obvious in 3 seconds.</li>
            </ul>
            <h3>Checklist</h3>
            <ul class="style-guide-checklist">
              <li>There is a clear visual hierarchy.</li>
              <li>Whitespace supports the message.</li>
              <li>Nothing feels cramped or noisy.</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="style-guide-section section-padding-sm" id="social-cards">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="row justify-content-center">
      <div class="col-12 col-lg-9">
        <h2>Social cards &amp; announcements</h2>
        <ul class="style-guide-summary">
          <li>Use repeatable templates: announcement, logistics, recap.</li>
          <li>Text-first layouts; no photos.</li>
          <li>Logo present but not dominant; date and location are clear.</li>
        </ul>
        <details class="accordion-minimal">
          <summary>Details, rationale, checklist</summary>
          <div class="style-guide-details-body">
            <h3>Announcement</h3>
            <ul>
              <li>Big headline + short subhead.</li>
              <li>Clear date and location line.</li>
              <li>Primary call to action in gold.</li>
            </ul>
            <h3>Logistics</h3>
            <ul>
              <li>Top line: event + date.</li>
              <li>Bulleted details: time, venue, registration link.</li>
              <li>Use purple for structure and dividers.</li>
            </ul>
            <h3>Recap</h3>
            <ul>
              <li>One short stat or takeaway.</li>
              <li>Location logo + small local highlight.</li>
              <li>Keep the layout calm and text-led.</li>
            </ul>
            <h3>Checklist</h3>
            <ul class="style-guide-checklist">
              <li>Template is consistent across locations.</li>
              <li>Text dominates, no photos.</li>
              <li>Action is clear and visually prioritized.</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="style-guide-section section-padding-sm" id="stickers">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="row justify-content-center">
      <div class="col-12 col-lg-9">
        <h2>Stickers, badges, swag</h2>
        <ul class="style-guide-summary">
          <li>Design for small formats; bold shapes, minimal text.</li>
          <li>Provide a monochrome variant for easy production.</li>
          <li>Limit colors and avoid fine detail.</li>
        </ul>
        <details class="accordion-minimal">
          <summary>Details, rationale, checklist</summary>
          <div class="style-guide-details-body">
            <h3>Print constraints</h3>
            <ul>
              <li>If it fails at 1 inch, simplify it.</li>
              <li>Use thick strokes and avoid tiny gaps.</li>
              <li>Keep to 2 to 4 colors max, including neutrals.</li>
            </ul>
            <h3>Checklist</h3>
            <ul class="style-guide-checklist">
              <li>Readable at small sizes.</li>
              <li>Monochrome variant supplied.</li>
              <li>No fragile detail or thin strokes.</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="style-guide-section section-padding-sm" id="deliverables">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="row justify-content-center">
      <div class="col-12 col-lg-9">
        <h2>Deliverables &amp; exports</h2>
        <ul class="style-guide-summary">
          <li>Always export SVG, PDF, and PNG (transparent + square).</li>
          <li>Include full color, 1-color black, and 1-color white.</li>
          <li>Use consistent naming with location and year.</li>
        </ul>
        <details class="accordion-minimal">
          <summary>Details, rationale, checklist</summary>
          <div class="style-guide-details-body">
            <h3>Required files</h3>
            <ul>
              <li>SVG (master)</li>
              <li>PDF (print)</li>
              <li>PNG transparent (large)</li>
              <li>PNG square (social avatar)</li>
              <li>1-color black and 1-color white variants</li>
            </ul>
            <h3>Naming convention</h3>
            <p><code>hoth-[location]-logo-[variant]-[YYYY].ext</code></p>
            <p>Example: <code>hoth-denver-logo-primary-2026.svg</code></p>
            <h3>Checklist</h3>
            <ul class="style-guide-checklist">
              <li>All required formats exported.</li>
              <li>Naming is consistent and searchable.</li>
              <li>Monochrome variants included.</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
