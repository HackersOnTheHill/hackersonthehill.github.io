FAQ

Who should attend?

Hackers on the Hill Denver is for cybersecurity researchers and practitioners who want to inform state policy with real-world experience. We also welcome policy-adjacent technologists who can contribute to technical discussions. This is a non-commercial event with a no-shilling policy.

Where is the event held?

We will meet at the Colorado State Capitol, 200 E Colfax Ave, Denver, CO 80203. The plenary discussion is in the Old State Library. Entry instructions and building access details will be shared closer to the event.

What is the schedule?

Times are TBD. Plan for a plenary discussion followed by small-group office briefings. We will publish the run of show as soon as it is confirmed.

How do I register?

You can register your interest here. Attendance is not guaranteed, and is limited by space and other factors. If you're approved you'll receive an email with more details.

What should I bring? What should I wear?

Bring a government-issued photo ID for security screening. A notepad is useful; laptops are optional. Dress comfortably and consider layers.

Will there be a happy hour?

Yes, we plan to host a happy hour afterward. Time and location are TBD.

Can I bring a guest?

Space is limited and everyone must register. Please do not bring unregistered guests.

What is the photo/video policy?

We will announce participation guidelines at the event. Please ask before taking photos. Expect the plenary to be on the record and the briefings to be off the record.
