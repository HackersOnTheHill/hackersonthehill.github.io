## About This Event

Hackers on the Hill DC is back for 2026, with an (almost) all new format. This ain't your grandparents' Hackers on the Hill. 

Building off the [Cyber Policy Shark Tank](/cyber-policy-shark-tank) ([Submit your idea!](https://forms.gle/M5WcMdFQT5LyT4V79)) experiments we did last year, we're coming back to [Critical Effect 2026](https://securityandtechnology.org/event/critical-effect-2026/) (June 17-18) as the closing session.

We're continuing the Congressional briefings, giving cybersecurity researchers the opportunity to engage with Congressional staffers in small, candid sessions, led by a Hacker Ambassador.

And **new** this year, a mock Congressional hearing. Congressional stand-ins (former Congressional staffers) will have the opportunity to grill a distinguished panel of invited experts on hot topics and torn-from-the headlines events. It's not one to be missed.

As always, the event is non-commercial. No vendor pitches, no lobbying, no sponsors taking the stage. Researchers and policymakers, working through the hard problems together.
